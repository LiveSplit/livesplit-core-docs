Class SumOfBestCleaner

A Sum of Best Cleaner allows you to interactively remove potential issues in the Segment History that lead to an inaccurate Sum of Best. If you skip a split, whenever you get to the next split, the combined segment time might be faster than the sum of the individual best segments. The Sum of Best Cleaner will point out all of occurrences of this and allows you to delete them individually if any of them seem wrong.
